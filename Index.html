Buffalo Chicken Dip

This stuff is incredible! So good.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient
1 Cup Canned Chicken
8 oz Cream Cheese
2 Cups Shredded Chicken Cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Place in a 15x9 pan
  3. Bake at 350 degrees
  4. Enjoy
